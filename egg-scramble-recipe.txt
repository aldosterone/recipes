Ingredients:
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 orange bell pepper, diced
- a handful of diced sundried tomatoes
- feta cheese
- garlic powder
- onion powder
- black pepper
- salt
- ground, crushed red pepper

Directions:
1. Scramble eggs
2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
3. Add the peppers and tomatoes.  Seanon with salt and pepepr.  Stir.  Cook a couple of minutes.
4. Add the feta cheese.
5. Add the spices.
6. Add the eggs and cook until desired consistency is reached.
